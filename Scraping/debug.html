Eredivisie Standings

The Eredivisie table, as we have already mentioned, includes 18 teams this season. The first four spots are available for European qualification, and the final two spots guarantee relegation, with 16th place having their fate decided by a playoff with an Eerste Divisie team.

Our Eredivisie standings page displays the current rankings, including points, games played, goals for and against, and goal difference. With the top four teams qualifying for Europe and the bottom two facing relegation, every detail counts, including the goal difference.
